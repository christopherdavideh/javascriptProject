Mean

The mean is the average of a data set.

Median

The median is the middle of the set of numbers.

Harmonic Mean

The harmonic mean is used quite a lot in physics. In some cases involving rates and ratios it gives a better average than the arithmetic mean. In addition, you’ll also find uses in geometry, finance and computer science.

Mode

The mode is the most common number in a data set.
